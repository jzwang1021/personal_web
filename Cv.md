## RESEARCH STATEMENT

Dr. Jianze Wang is an Associate Researcher specializing in structural resilience and intelligent disaster prevention. His work focuses on integrating **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Machine Learning (ML)** with traditional civil engineering for enhanced structural health monitoring, risk quantification, and hazard mitigation. He has successfully served as the Principal Investigator for numerous major projects, including those funded by the **National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)** and the **National Key R&D Program**. His research has resulted in over 60 academic papers, 18 patents/software copyrights, and significant technical application in Belt and Road energy infrastructure projects.
